PUC Case Management

What is a PUC Case?

A PUC case occurs when a customer decides to take legal action against the company or anything related to publicly damaging the company's reputation through social media, legal means, or communication media.

PUC also stands for Public Utility Commission of Texas. A complaint filed with this commission can be particularly damaging to the company.

Required Action

When dealing with a PUC case, you must transfer it to the specialized department called PUC Department.
